<div class="poem-body">
  <h2>What Distance Taught Us</h2>

  <p><em>The cherries shed their snow as years before,<br>
  And summer stars still tumble through the lake,<br>
  While autumn-gold chrysanthemums awake,<br>
  And winter plums bloom white along the shore.<br>
  The seasons keep their faith — but we who swore<br>
  To meet beneath each moon have let time break<br>
  What distance slowly taught us to forsake:<br>
  The same four views, two facing seats, one pour.</em></p>

  <p class="poem-body__closing"><em>“Spring remembered every petal, but forgot who I was waiting for.”</em></p>
